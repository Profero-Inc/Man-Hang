Prepare icon:
x86_64-w64-mingw32-windres gicon.rc -O coff -o gicon.res

Compile game:
x86_64-w64-mingw32-g++ Hangman_Windows.cpp Functions_Windows.cpp -I./PDCursesMod-master -L./PDCursesMod-master/wingui gicon.res -o Hangman.exe -lpdcurses -lwinmm -lgdi32 -lcomdlg32 -static-libstdc++ -mwindows -static

Remove mingw:
sudo pacman -Rcs mingw-w64-gcc
